3-Day Bangkok Itinerary

  • Day 1: Cultural Highlights
    1. Morning: Visit the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew
    2. Afternoon: Explore Wat Pho and get a Thai massage
    3. Evening: Enjoy street food and nightlife at Khao San Road
  • Day 2: Markets and Modern Attractions
    1. Morning: Relax at Lumphini Park
    2. Afternoon: Shop at Chatuchak Weekend Market
    3. Evening: Visit the Skywalk at King Power MahaNakhon for city views
  • Day 3: Unique Experiences
    1. Morning: Visit a floating market
    2. Afternoon: Enjoy a Thai massage
    3. Evening: Take a dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya River
